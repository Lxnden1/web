Frequently Asked Questions


What information does this website collect?

Nothing, we don't even track how many people download the client.

What is Fortress Client?

Fortress Client is a reskin of CheatBreaker, with way more features added!

Is it safe & free?

Yes! Fortress Client is safe and completely free to download.

How do I get cosmetics on the client?

By being active in our Discord. In doing so you will level up, and the higher the level, the more rewards you'll get!

How can I request for something to be added to Fortress Client?

You may suggest new features in our Discord.

Can I use my own mods?

No, you may only use mods included in Fortress Client.

Does Fortress Client boost frames?

Fortress Client will often more than double your frames in game.
